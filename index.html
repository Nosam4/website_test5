How to Play

The goal is to select the Secret Player!

To enter a guess, click either:

Click the Dropdown Arrow (↓) or type in the Player Name (Select Player)

Click SUBMIT to confirm your choice

The colors indicate the proximity to the Secret Player:

  • Green = Correct
  • Yellow = Close! Within Two Units
  • Blue = Correct Player

To play more, the RESET Button restarts the game with a New Secret Player. When typing in a Player Name, enter a space to see all players. Scroll to see all options.

Enjoy!
